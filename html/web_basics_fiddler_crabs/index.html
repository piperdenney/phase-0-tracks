Space Bears

Most people don't know that there are BEARS in SPACE

There are a few reasons a bear might venture to space

  • They may be hungry and searching for moon cheese
  • They may be going to verify that Pluto is indeed a planet
  • They may be seeking international fame

We asked one hundred bears about their favorite space destinations, here are the top 3!

  1. Pluto
  2. The moon (for the cheese of course!)
  3. Neptune
